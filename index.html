Modern Talking was a German pop duo that dominated the charts in the 80s and again in the late 90s. The group consisted of singer Thomas Anders and songwriter/producer Dieter Bohlen. Known for their catchy Europop tunes and energetic synth sounds, hits like "You're My Heart, You're My Soul" and "Cheri Cheri Lady" made them international stars. After a break-up in the late 80s, they had a successful reunion that brought them even more chart-topping singles before finally disbanding in 2003.
